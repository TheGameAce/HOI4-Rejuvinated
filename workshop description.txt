[h1]HOI4 Rejuvenated[/h1]
HOI4 Rejuvenated is a large-scale overhaul/content mod. It aims to overhaul a large amount of pre-existing game content, as well add a great deal to it.

[h1]New Content[/h1]
[img]https://i.imgur.com/BBXMeUY.png[/img]
[i]Countries with a New Focus Tree[/i]
[list]
[*]Generic Focus Tree - A brand new generic focus tree. A good bit more interesting than the base game's!
[*]New States - All across the world, with the exception of Europe. From the Americas & Madagascar, to Indonesia, Malaysia, & New Zealand.
[*]Resource Updates - Many countries now have more realistic & functional resources. New Zealand & South Africa are the coal havens they historically were. Cuba has a hearty supply of steel, representing historical (& notable) iron & copper mining. Central America is no longer a single steel or chromium here or there. Liberia even gets some love with annual rubber growth!
[*]Base Mechanic Changes - Smaller airwings (may be adjusted with feedback), more dockyards per ship, faster naval transports, reduced energy demands, building adjustments, faster wargoal justifications (may be tweaked in the future), and more!
[*]Global Population Overhaul - Tired of a decent chunk of world population being missing? Thanos couldn't keep everyone away forever, & they've been restored! (Though some countries did lose excess population)
[*]New Formables - Greater Caribbean Formable Nation - Some basic ideas of Caribbean unity floated around in the past. This is simply an expansion on that idea. Gain cores as any Caribbean sea-based country on all of Colombia, Venezuela, Panama, Central America, Belize, & Yucatan!
[*]Tech Tree Adjustments
[*]Unit Adjustments - Armored Cars have been made far more affordable, & much better at scouting & a few terrains.
[*]Misc. Country Tweaks (such as annual Liberian rubber events).
[*]Building Updates - Electrical grids, for instance, are no longer mutually exclusive, & no longer use a shared building slot with factories.
[*]Division Icon Additions - Credit to Alkolotl - This is an incorporation of his mod: Overwhelming Division Icons.
[/list]
